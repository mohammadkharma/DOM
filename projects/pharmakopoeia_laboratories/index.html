Our Directors

Abigail El-Doueik

MBBS, DFRSH, BSc Immunology

Medical Director

Haytham El-Doueik

B.Sc. Immunology Ph.D Medicine

Scientific Director

Haytham has a multi-disciplinary background that spans thirty years in medicinal herb growing, herbal extract preparations, use of herbal extracts in clinic settings, scientific background in immunology and finally translational medicine. In 2013 Haytham co-founded with his wife Dr Abigail their first integrated medicine clinic in Oxfordshire. Stamina

The long hours of training by elite athletes makes them more susceptible to upper respiratory tract infections. This can be attributed to the effects of strenuous exercise inducing a degree of immunosuppression. Our programmes aim at re-balancing the HPA axis which results in less sickness during training and competition season.

Recovery

Plastics and pesticides interact with ATP generation, the mitochondrial respiratory chain and DNA regulation. Other pollutants can reduce antioxidants such as glutathione lowering the recovery rate of athletes. Our detox programme aims to enhance detoxification via biohacking of several systems.

Performance

As more elite athletes suffer from psychological and gastrointestinal conditions that can be linked to the gut, there is a need for targeting the microbiota therapeutically with the correct prebiotics and probiotics. Our digestive programmes rebalance the microbiome to enhance mood, immune balance and digestion for maximising nutrient uptake.
